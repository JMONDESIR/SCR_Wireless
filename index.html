HOW IT WORKS

Simple and easy to understand

The latest technologies to provide our customers with the fastest speeds.

At SCRNCOM, we use the latest technologies to provide our customers with the fastest speeds and lowest ping. We achieve this by building radio towers in the local area that connect back to our main office. From these towers, a wireless signal is transmitted directly to each customer's home. A small 6-inch dish mounted outside the house receives the signal, delivering high-speed Internet right to your door.

To squash the rumors we do use 5Ghz signals to make the wireless connections. but these are not the same 5G from cell phone companies. 5G means 5th Generation and has nothing to do with the frequency in which it is transmitting. The "scary" frequency that is being used by the cell companies is in the 25-39GHz and that is much higher on the scale than we use. And the jury is still out on if the signal even causes issues in humans.
